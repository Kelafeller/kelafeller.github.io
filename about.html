WHAT WE DO

We are born with a mission to offer high-end design that can be produced without harming the natural balance of the earth. Our core values are to reduce the impact fashion has on the planet and to promote and educate for a transparent and sustainable business behavior. Our guiding and leading principles can be found in this manifest.

LONG TERM

Our long term approach guides us in all our processes. Every piece is designed to be timeless yet fashionable and have a thoughtful construction. Our materials are sourced to be of highest quality possible within our price category, we only work with manufactures that ensure the highest quality of work in order to create durable and long-lasting products which hopefully can be passed on for generations.

ZERO TOXINS
Never.

We aim to always prioritize materials that are pure, natural, plant based and naturally biodegrade. We avoid working with materials or practices that lead to social and environmental harm such as petroleum-based synthetics including polyester, acrylic, nylon, and other plastics. We always prioritize materials that are certified, recycled, organic and ethical sourced and produced. We do not use any toxic washes or dyes for our products and select the most environmentally friendly processes available.

Commitment
To Do Good

We want to promote and educate for a sustainable approach that inspire humanity to act in a responsible manner. Our practices shall never by any means harm animals, humans, or the environment. Instead, promote and contribute to a positive future for everyone.

CIRCULAR
Promote

A society with a circular economy meaning that nothing goes to waste and everything is utilized, reutilized, repurposed and recycled in the most effective, ethical and sustainable manner possible. Our business model is born on the idea to recycle and reconstruct overstocked surplus, vintage and materials in order to reduce waste.

Positive Cause
Together

We wish to be part of and to contribute to support, motivate and educate a circular, sustainable and ethical society where brands and organizations support positive causes, humanitarian, environmental, cultural or other which align with our core values. We are constantly looking to take actions to improve our performance in relation to the environmental agenda and stand beside others that do the same.
