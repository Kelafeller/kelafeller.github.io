A SUSTAINABLE LUXURY READY TO WEAR COLLECTION

MISSION

des REBELLES was created to promote change within the fashion industry. We support and raise awareness to protect the environment and eliminate pollution. We offer sustainable products, toxic free, plastic free made out of recycled fabrics and garments.

OUR STORY

We reinvent vintage military and over produced garments giving it a second life. Together with dead stock from natural fibers—100% silks and 100% cottons—we create sustainable luxury garments.

Both our wash or dyes are toxic free. Our eco conscious approach starts from the reinvention of the garment and fabric all the way to the final packaging. ”We want to reduce the environmental footprint in every step we take.

des REBELLES is a sustainable luxury brand in the pursuit to support the future of the environment. Determined to marry eco-conscious ethos with high style and quality, des REBELLES uses luxurious dead stock natural fibers 100% silks and 100% cottons—to reinvent military uniform overages and create modern silhouettes from vintage materials.

Founded in Los Angeles by Johanna Dauphin and Nathalie Lewin, Des Rebelles collections offers high quality re-constructed one of a kind pieces using the finest material, impeccable tailoring and detailing.
