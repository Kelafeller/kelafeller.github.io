SHIPPING POLICY

Domestic Shipping

All U.S. orders qualify for 25 US$ Standard Shipping. If you place a domestic order for over 300 US$ you qualify for free shipping/ Orders are typically processed within 3 days of receipt if the item is in stock. If you purchased an item that is out of stock it will take 3-4 weeks for your order to be completed. We will contact you via email if there are any issues with your orders. For all shipping inquiries, please email us at hello@desrebelles.com We insure all packages against theft and accidental damage while in transit. Once the package is delivered and signed for, we are no longer liable. Orders cannot be delivered to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses.

International Shipping

All international orders will be subject to shipping charges, taxes, customs, duties, and fees based upon the destination country and the item(s) ordered. The recipient of the shipment is the importer on record in the destination country and is responsible for all import fees. All other orders will be shipped fully insured.

In placing an order, you authorize Des Rebelles to designate a carrier to act as your agent with the relevant customs and tax authorities in the destination country, to clear your merchandise, process and remit your actual import fees for your item(s). If you refuse a shipment from Des Rebelles, you are responsible for the original shipping charges, any import fees that are incurred on the package, and the cost of returning the package to Des Rebelles.
