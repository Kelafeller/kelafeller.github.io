PACKAGING

We are always avoiding any kinds of single use plastic in our packaging and wrapping. We choose the most sustainable environmentally friendly modes to safely delivering our product to you.

We aim towards full transparency of our supply chains and are constantly searching to identify and build long lasting relationships with suppliers and partners that are or want to be part of the green revolution.
